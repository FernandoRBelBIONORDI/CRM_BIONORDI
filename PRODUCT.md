# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Biomedical equipment sales technicians and service staff at Bionordi, a Mexican company. They use the CRM daily to manage leads (prospectos), repair orders (taller), and quotations. Workflow is split between desktop at the office and mobile in the field. Primary tasks on any given screen: check today's follow-up queue, update a lead's status, create or view a repair order.

## Product Purpose

Bionordi CRM centralizes prospecting, pipeline management, workshop repair tracking, and PDF quotation generation for a biomedical equipment business. Success means the team spends less time on admin and more time closing and servicing clients.

## Brand Personality

Precise, Trustworthy, Efficient. The interface should feel like a well-calibrated instrument: structured and dependable, not clinical or sterile. Confidence without coldness.

## Anti-references

- EHR / hospital management software: white + teal, form-heavy layouts, patient-record grids, EMR aesthetics.
- Generic enterprise CRM: Salesforce / HubSpot blue-top-nav, dense sidebar, corporate bloat.
- Consumer health apps: pastel gradients, rounded pill aesthetics, "wellness" warmth.

## Design Principles

1. **Instrument-grade clarity**: every screen answers one primary question at a glance. No decorative noise that competes with the data.
2. **Status at a distance**: urgency and status must be readable from a phone in poor light. Color carries semantic weight, not just decoration.
3. **Efficient over impressive**: reduce friction for repeat daily actions (check queue, log contact, advance pipeline) over visual showmanship.
4. **Trustworthy defaults**: the app handles business-critical data. Confidence comes from reliable behavior and visual consistency, not trends.
5. **Mobile without compromise**: field use on phones is a primary scenario, not an afterthought. Touch targets, readable type, and glanceable layout at all times.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum + mobile-first priority. Touch targets 44x44px minimum throughout. The team uses the app on phones in the field; font sizes, contrast, and tap targets must work without zooming. Reduced motion: respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
